Watch Facts

  • Rocketpiece: Booster
  • Color: Orange (original)
  • Material: Original rocketmaterial
  • Flightheight: 45 Km+, Mesosphere

The plate is milled directly from the rocket, the original color and texture (scratches, traces of paint) are unchanged. On closer inspection, each plate is different due to various scratches - and is therefore unique.

It left our planet and touched the edge of space at an altitude of 45 km.

Mission Facts

  • Mission: Soyuz MS-09
  • Operator: Roscosmos
  • Duration: 196d 17h 50m
  • Mission Date: 6 June 2018 - 20 December 2018
  • Spacecraft: Soyuz-MS
  • Rocket: Soyuz-FG

Rockets fly into space in multiple stages (Soyuz has with 3). The first stage will separate an altitude of 45 km and 85 km. It then falls into the uninhabited steppe of Kazakhstan. Stage 3 reaches orbit at an altitude of around 200 km. From this altitude, the spacecraft flies to the International Space Station at an altitude of about 400 km. It docked there 2 days after liftoff on June 8, 2018,

There is no fixed boundary to space. The transition of the earth's atmosphere is fluid. The Fédération Aéronautique International (FAI) has followed Kármán's definition and set the limit at 100 km altitude, the Kármán line. For the US Air Force (USAF), space begins at 80 km.
